Massachusetts Bay Transportation Authority

Fares & Passes

The T offers many different transport services, and they all have different fares:

Subway/Streetcar

Green, Red, Blue, & Orange Lines

  • $2.40 with CharlieCard
  • $2.90 with CharlieTicket or cash

CharlieCard fare offers 1 free transfer to local bus within two hours of payment.

Local Bus

  • $1.70 with CharlieCard
  • $2.00 with CharlieTicket or cash

CharlieCard fare offers 1 free transfer to local bus, within two hours of payment, or pay the difference for transfer to subway.

Express Bus

Inner Express

  • $4.25 with CharlieCard
  • $5.25 with CharlieTicket or cash

Outer Express Express

  • $5.25 with CharlieCard
  • $7.00 with CharlieTicket or cash

CharlieCard fare offers 1 free transfer to local bus or subway, within two hours of payment.

Commuter Rail

  • $2.40 - $13.25 with CharlieTicket or mTicket app

Distance-based fare. No free transfers.

Ferry

  • $3.70 - $9.75 with Paper Ticket or mTicket app

Fare varies by route. No free transfers.
